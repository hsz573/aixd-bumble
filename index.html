Prototype Guide

The Sender POV drives the flow. The Receiver POV updates reactively to show the other side.

  1. 1

    Complete the onboarding

    You're here. Finish the onboarding flow — it triggers a notification on the Crossed Paths widget.

  2. 2

    Enter Crossed Paths

    Tap the yellow widget on the Discover screen to navigate to crossed-paths.html.

  3. 3

    Message your crossed paths match

    Open the chat with the match you crossed paths with and send a message — it appears in the receiver's chat list.

  4. 4

    Like a potential match

    Tap a card → open the profile → tap Like. A dot appears on the receiver's Liked You tab.

  5. 5

    Match from the receiver

    On the receiver, tap Liked You → reveal → tap Match to see the match screen.
